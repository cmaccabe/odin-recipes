Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • Two pounds stewing beef
  • Three tablspns flour
  • Half tspn garlic powder
  • Half tspn black pepper
  • Three tblspns olive oil
  • Six cup beef broth

Steps

  1. Combine garlic flour and pepper. Toss beef in flour mixture
  2. Heat olive oil in large pot
  3. Add in all ingredients and stir regularly up to ninety minutes
